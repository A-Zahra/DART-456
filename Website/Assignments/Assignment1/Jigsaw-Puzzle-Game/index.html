Level 1 [EAZY]

INSTRUCTION

  1. To activate puzzle screen mouse over the white space.
  2. To place puzzle pieces:
    1. Click on the empty spot you want to drop a piece to.
    2. Drag the piece from the slider and drop it to the place.
  3. DON'T click on more than one empty spot at a time as it may drop the piece to the both!
  4. If the piece you have taken isn't the correct one,you can put it back to its slide and take another piece.
  5. NOTICE that once you drop a piece to its SPOT, it won't be draggable any more!

Level 2 [HARD]

INSTRUCTION

  1. You have 15 questions in total. Every 17 seconds a new question pops up.
  2. You have 15 seconds to answer the question.
  3. If your answer is wrong or you don't answer, the next question pops up right after.
  4. To win the game you need to complete the puzzle before the list of questions is finished!
  5. Puzzle pieces are only dropped to the right empty spots.
  6. Pay attention to the clues. Sometimes they are part of the answer!
  7. To answer some of the questions, you need to search on Google.
