Favorite Organization

ON animation studios is an independent European leading studio with offices in Montreal, Paris, and Los Angeles. It specializes in television series and feature film production.
The animation by which I came to know this studio is called "The little prince". As stated on their website, this animation is "still the most successful French animated movie in the world". Some of the most noticeable awards that this animation movie has won are:
  1. Bafta-2017(Best feature film)
  2. Cesar-2016(Best animated movie)
  3. Annie Awards-2017(Outstanding Achievement
  4. Music in an animated feature production)
Producing award-winning animations like "The little prince", "Mune", and "The Prodigies" is the first significant feature of this company that motivates me to think of it as one of the places I would like to work at in the future. Moreover, the ON animation studios' team professionalism and expertise that is visible through their impressive storytelling style and high-quality animations can be another large incentive for students like me to want to work at their company after graduation. Finally, after reviewing "The Little Prince" brief description on the ON animation studios website, I realized that this work's director has been Mr. Mark Osborne, a very successful and well-known director in the world of animation film production. Working in a company that provides you with the opportunity to collaborate with experts, leading figures in your favorite field, and participate in large projects that have the chance to win awards can be any novice dream.
